static string Topos.NumberTheory.Fibonacci.Zeckendorf ( Integer  n)
static

Rewrites a positive integer n in its Zeckendorf representation. Zeckendorf presentation of n is of the form of sum of Fibonacci numbers where no terms are consecutive. A Zeckendorf representation is unique for integer n up to their ordering.
